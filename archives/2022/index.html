About

I am joining ByteDance, Ltd. in Spring 2024 as a full-time Machine Learning Platform R&D Engineer in the Applied Machine Learning(AML) department. My BSc in Computer Science at New York University Shanghai will be conferred soon.

I am actively looking for research opportunity in Distributed Systems and Networks, I am also willing to explore in PL/SE for Systems. Specifically, I am currently interested in effective and verifiable cluster management (e.g. placement, load-balancing, update, migration), building easy-to-use and extensible acceleration techniques (e.g. optimizing usage of eBPF, smartNIC), and improving the observability of complicated microservices (e.g. tracing, kernel tracepoints).

I have been working as a research assistant at NYUSH since winter 2021, participating in two papers under preparation in secure network management, for which I actively contribute in many aspects including system implementation, algorithm design, evaluations, etc. I have been working on fault tolerance for Service Mesh at NYU from summer 2023.

Contact me through the email address in the menu.
